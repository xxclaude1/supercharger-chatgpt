## Title
Supercharger for ChatGPT — Fix Lag & Freezing in Long Chats

## Short Description
Stop browser freezing and lag in long ChatGPT conversations. Instantly speeds up the entire UI and reduces memory usage. 100% free.

## Detailed Description

Stop ChatGPT from freezing and lagging — instantly, for free.

Supercharger for ChatGPT makes massive conversations smooth again by rendering only the most recent messages at a time. No lost context, no data collection, no slowdown — and no price tag.

If you use ChatGPT for coding, writing, research, or long discussions, you know the pain: once a chat gets long, Chrome slows to a crawl, scrolling becomes jittery, and CPU usage spikes.

Supercharger fixes this instantly.


🚀 What It Does

Makes ChatGPT fast again — even in threads with 500+ messages — by rendering only the latest set of messages. You choose exactly how many to keep visible.

Older messages are easily restored: just scroll up and press "Load more messages." Your full chat history stays intact and accessible — we just stop your browser from choking on it.


✅ Why You'll Love It

- Smooth, zero-lag typing & scrolling — even in massive threads
- Lower CPU & memory usage — keeps Chrome fast and extends battery life
- No lost messages — your complete history stays intact on OpenAI's servers
- Works automatically — install once and ChatGPT instantly feels faster
- Fully configurable — set exactly how many messages to keep visible
- 100% free — no paid tier, no limits, no "upgrade" nags. Ever.


🔒 100% Private

All processing happens entirely on your device. Your chat data never leaves your browser.
No data collection. No tracking. No analytics. No uploads. No chat deletions — ever.

The extension only has permission to run on chatgpt.com and cannot see, access, or touch any other website, tab, or file on your computer.


🎁 100% Free — No Catch

Unlike other extensions that limit free users or charge for full functionality, Supercharger is completely free with zero limits. No daily caps, no feature gates, no "PRO" upsell. Every feature is available to every user from day one.

This is an open-source project. You can audit every line of code on GitHub.


🛠 How It Works

The extension renders only the most recent messages to keep your browser fast. Need to see older parts of the conversation? Just scroll up and hit "Load more." Your full history remains safely on OpenAI's servers — we just make it easier for your browser to handle.


❤️ A Note from the Developer

This tool eliminates browser rendering lag — the local slowdowns that happen when ChatGPT tries to render hundreds of messages at once. It can't fix OpenAI's server-side issues, but it makes a massive difference for the lag you experience on your end.

This is an open-source project built to solve a problem that's bothered ChatGPT users for years. If it helps you, a 5-star review goes a long way.
